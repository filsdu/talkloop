TalkLoop Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

  • What we collect: Your phone number, RSVP or status commands (e.g. "join", "online"), call participation (e.g. who you were matched with).
  • What we do: We use this data to route calls, track usage, and send reminders. Feedback ratings are used to monitor system quality.
  • What we don't do: We never sell your data. We don't record voice calls. No one outside TalkLoop can access your information.
  • You can leave at any time by texting "remove me". We will delete your status and RSVP data.

This policy may be updated for transparency or compliance reasons. Last updated: July 2025.

Terms of Use – TalkLoop

TalkLoop Terms of Use

  • TalkLoop is a peer support system — not a dating platform or therapy provider.
  • All users must respect each other: no flirting, harassment, or sharing personal info.
  • You must be at least 16 years old to use this platform.
  • TalkLoop reserves the right to block users for violations of our Respect Code or abusive behavior.
  • Calls are anonymous and not stored. Usage may be logged to prevent misuse.

By using TalkLoop, you agree to these terms. Last updated: July 2025.
